History

TechCon was founded in 2010 with the mission of connecting tech enthusiasts and professionals to foster innovation and share cutting-edge advancements. From a small meetup of 100 attendees, TechCon has grown into one of the most influential global technology events.

Mission

TechCon 2024 is committed to fostering inclusivity, advancing education, and empowering developers and leaders from around the world. We believe in the transformative power of technology to solve global problems.
